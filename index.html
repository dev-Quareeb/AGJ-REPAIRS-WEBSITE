Ask Us Directly 💬
  • How long does a typical phone repair take? +

    Most repairs like screen or battery replacement take between 30 minutes to 1 hour, depending on the issue and device model.

  • What if I don't know what's wrong with my device? +

    No worries — bring it in or book a diagnostic service. We'll assess it and let you know the issue before any repair begins.

  • Do you use original parts? +

    We use high-quality OEM or original parts depending on availability and your preference.

  • What payment methods do you accept? +

    We accept cash, bank transfers, and mobile payments!

  • Where are you located? +

    We are located in Ogun State, but we have technicians across Nigeria.

  • Do you offer a warranty? +

    We offer a limited lifetime warranty on repairs. If the original repair fails due to a defect in the part we installed or workmanship, we will fix it for you.

  • Do you manufacture the parts you use? +

    No, we are not a manufacturing company, but the materials we use are from the best manufacturers in the industry!
